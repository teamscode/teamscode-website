## Chen Chao - Principal Engineering Manager, Microsoft Co-Founder & Board Member, Career Up Club Expert in AI, ML, distributed systems and security

<img src="/images/speakers/chaochen.webp" height="200px" style="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:10px">

Chao Chen is a Principal Engineering Manager at Microsoft, where he leads teams building large-scale security infrastructure and advancing next-generation, agentic data center management systems. He brings deep expertise across machine learning, artificial intelligence, distributed systems, and security, with a strong track record of delivering reliable, high-impact platforms at scale. Beyond his work at Microsoft, Chao is a co-founder and board member of Career Up Club and a former president of the Seattle Entrepreneurship Club, and he is passionate about empowering engineers and leaders to navigate career growth, embrace emerging technologies, and build meaningful impact.
